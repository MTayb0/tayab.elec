About us

Your Trusted Electrical & Automation Experts, we bring innovation, expertise, and reliability to every project. Founded by a certified Electrical Engineer with years of hands-on experience in home automation, villas, building line installations, and complete electrical solutions, our company is dedicated to delivering top-quality services to residential and commercial clients alike.

Our approach combines technical excellence with strategic planning to ensure optimal performance, safety, and energy efficiency in every installation. From bespoke villa electrification to commercial-grade automation systems, projects are executed using industry-leading practices and premium components for enduring reliability.

No job is too big or small for our skilled team

Services

Professional Electrical & Automation Services

  • Comprehensive Electrical Installations

    Engineered electrical systems for villas, apartments, and commercial properties, including: Full wiring and circuit design, Distribution board installations, Lighting and power point configurations, and Compliance with local electrical code

  • Smart Home & Building Automation

    Integrated intelligent systems for modern living and workspaces: Automated lighting control systems, Smart climate management (HVAC integration), Voice-controlled and app-based automation(KNX, Zigbee, DALI, IOT, Home Assistant), and Multi-room audio-visual solutions

  • Electrical Maintenance & Upgrades

    Proactive system optimization and troubleshooting: Fault diagnosis and circuit repairs, Electrical safety inspections, Energy efficiency improvements, and Legacy system modernization

  • Structured Network Infrastructure

    Professional-grade connectivity solutions: Enterprise Wi-Fi network design, Wired LAN installations, Data cabling (Cat6/Cat6a), and Network security configurations

  • Security & Surveillance Systems

    Tailored protection solutions: IP CCTV system design/installation, Access control systems, Alarm and intrusion detection, and Remote monitoring integration

  • Custom Electrical Solutions

    Specialized services for unique requirements: Control panels, Backup power systems (UPS) , Home theater wiring, Electrical design consultation, Pool DBs, Gate motors, Forward reverse motors, Motor starters, and Motor Controls.

Why choose us?

    Certified Expertise – Fully licensed with ongoing professional development in electrical engineering and automation systems

    Integrated Solutions – Seamlessly combine traditional electrical work with smart technology integration

    Project Accountability – Direct oversight of all installations with rigorous quality control protocols

    Regulatory Compliance – All work meets or exceeds local electrical codes and safety standards
